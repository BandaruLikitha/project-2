Our Pure and Natural Milk

At Go Matha Dairy Farm, we take pride in delivering milk that is of the highest quality, naturally fresh, and pure. Our cows are raised in a clean, healthy environment and are provided with a well-balanced diet.

How We Naturally Produce Milk

  • Traditional Farming: Our cows are fed natural, non-GMO feed suited to each species.
  • Regular Health Checkups: Our cows are routinely examined by veterinarians.
  • Hygienic Milking Process: Our milking equipment is regularly cleaned and sanitized.
  • Immediate Cooling: The milk is instantly cooled after milking to maintain freshness.
