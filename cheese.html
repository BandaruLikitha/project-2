Our Handmade Cheese

At Go Matha Dairy Farm, we create cheese using traditional methods. Using high-quality milk from our cows, we craft all-natural and delicious cheeses.

How We Naturally Make Cheese

  • Traditional Methods: We follow time-honored techniques in our cheese-making process.
  • Natural Ingredients: We use natural rennet and cultures, avoiding artificial additives.
  • Handcrafted Process: We ensure top quality by making our cheese in small batches.
  • Rich Flavor: Our cheeses are aged under optimal conditions for the best taste.
